About Our Workshop

Engineering Excellence Since Decades

Hina Machinery Centre is a leading engineering workshop based in Ghaziabad, Uttar Pradesh. We specialize in the manufacturing, supply, and servicing of mixer machines, concrete equipment, fabrication tools, and custom industrial machinery.

Located on the Delhi–Meerut Expressway, we cater to construction companies, contractors, and industrial clients across North India. Our workshop is equipped with skilled craftsmen who ensure every machine meets stringent quality standards.
