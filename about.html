Our Story

Hina Machinery Centre

Hina Machinery Centre has been a trusted name in engineering works and machinery manufacturing for over two decades. Based in Ghaziabad, Uttar Pradesh, on the Delhi–Meerut Expressway, we are strategically located to serve clients across Delhi NCR and the entire North Indian belt.

Our workshop specializes in manufacturing concrete mixer machines, self-loading mobile mixers, vibratory plate compactors, chaff cutter machines, and custom fabricated industrial equipment. Each product is built with precision by our skilled team of engineers and craftsmen.

We take pride in our ability to deliver custom machinery tailored to the specific needs of our clients — whether it's a small contractor needing a single mixer or an industrial company requiring bulk orders. Our workshop is equipped with modern fabrication tools, welding equipment, and quality testing facilities.

Our commitment to quality, competitive pricing, and after-sales support has made us the preferred machinery supplier for hundreds of construction companies and contractors across India.
